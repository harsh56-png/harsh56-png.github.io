THE FAMILY MAN

        

The Hollywood cast and content surely would have influenced our mind and hearts, but when it comes to raw and desi touch of the bollywood creators no one can ignore the fact that it has a separate fan level in the hearts of people. Reviewing today the top class category of our very own bollywood web series that are famous for its raw content and strong language, famous for being the best in crime thrillers list here we are with “THE FAMILY MAN”

Ratings

IMDb: 8.6/10

Movie Matrix: 8.8/10

We all live in a world where some of us openly share our problems and other life moments with our closed ones, but on the other, we also have certain of us, who tend to remain self centered and keep our things to ourselves. “The Family Man” is the series depecting the scenarios of the second kind of situation. This crime thriller series of unexpected moments ad outstanding acting is what makes it worth watching. The series contains a total of 10 episodes with a rocking flavor of uniqueness filled entirely in each episode. Released in 2019, the series has been directed by “Krishna D.K.” and “Raj Nidimoru”, with the excellent plot of “Sumit Arora”, and the cast containing the evergreen acting of “Manoj Bajpayee” the others including “Sharib Hashmi”, “Priyamani” and others. The plot of the series is no simple scenario of a local thief police chase, rather is the high scale planning of terror attacks. The series shows the viewers a real image of how a man in the intelligence bureau, facing death everyday in his life, keeps everything away from his family in order to live a simple and normal life. The series becomes a hit with every punch of Manoj Bajpayee's acting, along with the moments if comedy which the series include at frequent intervals, makes it even better to watch. Keeping apart the scenarios at home, the series depicts another phase of a person’s life dealing with extreme terror and removing threats to his country. Working in a government sector, the series also shares the scenarios that a government servant is a 24 hour serving person with a minimal pay he gets to run his life. The series surely gives us some wow moments and hooking up till the end of the series. To be honest, the series does not bore you at all, and one is happily a part of it while watching. As the series moves towards the end, the plot keeps on getting more and more deep thus making it even more exciting and interesting to handle. The series has surely left the ends unclosed and thus owes its viewers a second season. All together, the series is a must watch and is the most entertaining one this list of crime and thrills. So, go on, stream the life of a common Family Man with the ups and downs and the happy and exciting moments.
