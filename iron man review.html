MARVEL'S: IRON MAN

        

Ever wondered how a Sci-Fi fiction with adventures and action would be like? Well, one must watch such movies as they settle deep into our minds and one cannot stop thinking about it for days. Moreover, a mixture of action including an epic show of advanced technology attracts you in the most unique and amazing way. As mentioned in the title, MARVEL STUDIOS brings you a complete package of such movies,including one such gem the "IRON MAN" movie series including 3 parts. Reviewing all the 3 parts sequentially, here is what the movie brings to its viewers:

IRON MAN (2008)



        

Ratings

IMDb: 7.9/10

Movie Matrix: 8.5/10

Bringing to its viewers an amazing combination of action and technology the first part of IRON MAN is a thrill to watch. Having written by "Mark Fergus","Hawk Ostby" and based on the comics by "Stan Le", the movie is directed by "Jon Favreau". The movie extends to a duration of 2 hours 6 min and was released in 2008. Also containing an amazing cast including "Robert Downey Jr.", " Gwyneth Paltrow" and many others, the outstanding acting with a mixture of slight comedy by Robert will make you love him till infinity. The movie's main idea is the discovery of a highly advanced IRON MAN suit and how it changes the entire world with its existence. Since, no movie is complete without a villain,the movie on contrast shares the idea that technology can be useful and harmful for mankind. The movie definitely contains many Wow and hooking up moments where one is lost into the IRON MAN world and doesn't really wanna come out of it. Being just the beginning/just the first part, the movie mostly focuses on the building and coming into being of the IRON MAN. Not to take it negatively, this concept is just more than enough to hook anyone for the entire duration. Thus, summing up the things , IRON MAN doesn't bore you even in the least of the ways and is highly recommended for everyone, especially for the Sci-Fi movie lovers.

IRON MAN 2 (2010)



        

Ratings

IMDb: 7.0/10

Movie Matrix: 7.2/10

Talking about part 2, it is pretty sure that one would have been addicted to the IRON MAN universe till now. So, the second part of the IRON MAN attains its name in the most common sequel form as "IRON MAN 2". T h e movie comes up with another amazing script and direction by "Stan Le" and "Jon Favreau", extending up to a duration of 2 hours 4 min and was released in 2010. The movie though is a long gone release of 2010, it is still in hearts of people because of a different level craziness for IRON MAN. The cast includes all the same characters with the introduction of another MARVEL gem "BLACK WIDOW" casting "Scarlett Johansson". Though being a sequel of the IRON MAN series, the MARVEL world always gives its viewers a reason to love them.The movie contains some extremely beautifully designed moments along with an epic story line.s the movie introduces a new villain with an extraordinary tech mechanism being used for destruction. "IRON MAN 2" again shares the amazing battle between good and evil,along with bringing its viewers an amazing show of video edits , cinematography and screenplay. Moreover, our most beloved IRON MAN takes acting to another level. Though being a sequel of the IRON MAN series, the MARVEL world always gives its viewers a reason to love them. The movie contains some extremely beautifully designed moments along with an epic story line. No doubts, if you have come to the second part of the IRON MAN movie series, you are surely addicted to it till now. Also, introducing a new concept of JARVIS as an A.I in the movie, takes it another step towards excellence. Finally gathering things together, the movie again binds you up giving you no reason to hate it, thus making it a beautiful thing to invest time in.

IRON MAN 3 (2013)



        

Ratings

IMDb: 7.2/10

Movie Matrix: 7.5/10

Happily locked up and never wanting to escape from the IRON MAN world, here you are with the third part of the IRON MAN series. The destruction here is enormous, believe it. Working with the sequel, the IRON MAN's third movie is literally "IRON MAN 3". Written and directed by "Shane Black"and others, the movie contains an enormous amount of action, packed tightly inside it, where once stuck, no one wishes to escape. The movies cast consist of again the same characters including "Robert Downey Jr.", "Jon Favreau", "Gwyneth Paltrow" and many others. The movie being a slight longer than the other two parts extends to a duration of 2 hours 10 min and was released in 2013. The finale of IRON MAN distributes an entire sky filled with action and destruction. The movie brings something very unexpected as the Stark House is blown to pieces by Mandarin. Thus, leading to a comeback of a much advanced version of the IRON MAN. This sets another level of fandom for IRON MAN in its fans. Coming from its journey to the last part, IRON MAN now has made a separate fan base in the MARVEL fans hearts. The movie binds you up with action and adventure and how the existence of IRON MAN is now a need of the world. The finale heads to the enhancement of the IRON MAN powers, the most loved superhero in the realistic world. The movie is again a must recommended to be watched, and enjoyed, giving you thrills, goosebumps, pleasure,entertainment and what not. Go, watch that out.
